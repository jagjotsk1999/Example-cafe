Recipies-World's best scrambled eggs

I adapted this recipe from a book called Sydney Food by Bil Granger. Ever since tasting these eggs on my first visit to Bil's restaurant in kings cross, sydney, i have been after the recipe. i have since transformed it into what i really believe are the best scrambled eggs i have ever tasted.
This recipe is what i call a 'very special breakfast'. just look at the ingredients to see why it has to be tasted to be believed.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving:
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream
  • a pinch of salt
  • freshly miled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives(chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, cream and salt in a bowl.
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a high heat.
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait until it starts setting around the edge of the pan.
  4. Using a wooden spatula, bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omlet, and let it cook for another 20 seconds.
  5. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly miled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives.
You should only make a maximum of two servings per frying pan.
